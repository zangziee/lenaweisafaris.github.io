About Lenawei Safaris

Our Story

Lenawei Safaris was founded by explorers driven by a passion for Africa’s natural beauty and a mission to create life-changing journeys. We started in 2010 as a small, family-run business, and today we are proud to be Africa’s leading safari operator with thousands of happy guests from around the world.

Our Mission

  • To connect travelers with Africa’s wildlife, culture, and people through ethical, immersive experiences.
  • To protect the environment and support local communities with every journey.
  • To deliver personalized service and lasting memories for every guest.

Our Values

  • Respect for nature and local cultures
  • Integrity and transparency
  • Innovation and flexibility
  • Safety first, always

Company History

  1. 2010: Lenawei Safaris is founded in Nairobi, Kenya.
  2. 2012: Expansion to Tanzania and Uganda; first eco-certified tours launched.
  3. 2015: Winner, National EcoTourism Award.
  4. 2018: Over 1,000 guests served; new offices in Botswana and South Africa.
  5. 2022: TripAdvisor Travelers’ Choice Award winner.

Our Impact

Education

10% of profits go to school scholarships, books, and supplies for local children in our host communities.

Conservation

Partnerships with anti-poaching units and wildlife sanctuaries—over $100K donated since 2015.

Clean Water

We help fund wells and safe water projects in rural villages near our destinations.

Local Jobs

90% of our staff are from local communities. We provide fair wages, training, and growth opportunities.

Our Sustainability in Numbers

  • +50,000 trees planted
  • 3,200 students supported
  • 20 community projects funded
  • Zero single-use plastics on tours
